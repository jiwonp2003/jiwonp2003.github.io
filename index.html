Featured Project

Forest Age Mapping — City of Atlanta

Developed a geospatial analysis pipeline to estimate forest age across Atlanta, supporting urban planning and ecological decision-making.

What I Did:

  • Georeferenced historical aerial imagery using QGIS
  • Processed and mosaiced raster datasets for city-wide coverage
  • Resolved CRS mismatches (NAD83 vs Web Mercator)
  • Cleaned NoData artifacts to improve data quality
  • Integrated data from USGS EarthExplorer for temporal analysis

Impact:

Supports the City of Atlanta in evaluating land parcels for acquisition based on forest maturity and ecological value.
